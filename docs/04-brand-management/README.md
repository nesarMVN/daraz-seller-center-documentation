# Brand Management Documentation

## Overview

The **Brand Management** page is the central hub for managing brand authorizations and monitoring DarazMall eligibility in the Daraz Seller Center. Sellers can view their brand relationships, check transfer criteria for DarazMall status, and manage their authorized brands.

**URL:** `https://sellercenter.daraz.com.bd/portal/apps/setting/brand_v2`

**Navigation Path:** Products > Brand Management

---

## Page Layout

### Header Section

The Brand Management header displays:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Page Title** | "Brand Information" - main heading |
| **Breadcrumb** | Homepage > Setting > Brand |
| **Authenticity Policy Alert** | Blue information box with DarazMall policies |

### Important: DarazMall Authenticity Policy

A prominent blue alert box explains DarazMall's authenticity guarantee:

**Key Points:**
- **100% product authenticity** guaranteed to customers
- - Only **brand owners** or **authorized distributors** with valid proof can list products
  - - Products with the **DarazMall tag** come with **money-back guarantee** if counterfeit
    - - **Inauthentic products will be delisted** and **3x penalty** charged for each return
      - - Sellers must ensure all products meet authenticity requirements

        ## Main Tabs

        The Brand Management page has two primary tabs:

        ### 1. Transfer Criteria Tab (Default)

        This tab displays the seller's eligibility for DarazMall status based on performance metrics.

        #### DarazMall Eligibility Status

        **Status Display:**
        - Shows current eligibility status (e.g., "Daraz Mall Eligibility: Failed")
        - - Visual indicator with warning icon
          - - Color-coded status (orange/yellow for failed)
           
            - #### Performance Criteria Table
           
            - The transfer criteria table includes the following columns:
           
            - | Column | Description |
            - |--------|-------------|
            - | **Criteria** | Name of performance metric |
            - | **Definition** | Detailed explanation of how metric is calculated |
            - | **Passing Criteria** | Minimum required threshold |
            - | **Current Performance** | Seller's current performance value |
            - | **Meets Criteria** | Yes/No indicator (checkmark or dash) |
           
            - #### Performance Metrics
           
            - **1. Seller Rating (Last 90 Days)**
            - - **Definition:** The ratio of total positive ratings to total ratings from verified buyers during the assessment cycle
              - - **Passing Criteria:** At least 70%
                - - **Assessment Period:** Last 90 days
                  - - **Purpose:** Measures customer satisfaction with products and service
                   
                    - **2. Cancellation Rate (L30D)**
                    - - **Definition:** The ratio of items cancelled because of seller cancellations or when platform cancelled because the seller did not ship the item on time to total items ordered during assessment cycle
                      - - **Passing Criteria:** Less than 1%
                        - - **Assessment Period:** Last 30 days (L30D)
                          - - **Purpose:** Measures reliability in fulfilling orders
                           
                            - **3. Ship on Time Rate (L30D)**
                            - - **Definition:** The ratio of ordered items, from the time the order is received, that are picked up within 24 hours and handed over to logistics provider to total items ordered during assessment cycle
                              - - **Passing Criteria:** At least 75%
                                - - **Assessment Period:** Last 30 days (L30D)
                                  - - **Purpose:** Measures shipping efficiency and timeliness
                                   
                                    - **4. Same day Response rate (L30D)**
                                    - - **Definition:** The ratio of received conversation that are replied in 24 hours to total conversation received during assessment cycle
                                      - - **Assessment Period:** Last 30 days (L30D)
                                        - - **Passing Criteria:** At least 70%
                                          - - **Purpose:** Measures customer service responsiveness
                                           
                                            - #### Performance Data Tips
                                           
                                            - A "Performance Data Tips" link is provided below the table for additional guidance on improving metrics.

                                                                                                                                                                                                       9. ## DarazMall Transfer Criteria Details
                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                       10. ### Understanding the Metrics
                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                       11. **Assessment Cycles:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                       12. - Different metrics use different time periods
                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - L30D = Last 30 Days
                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Last 90 Days for seller rating
                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Metrics are calculated continuously
                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - **Meeting All Criteria:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - ALL four metrics must meet passing criteria simultaneously
                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Falling below threshold on any metric = DarazMall ineligibility
                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Regular monitoring recommended to maintain status
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - **Improvement Strategies:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - **For Seller Rating (70%):**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Ensure product quality matches descriptions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Provide excellent customer service
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Respond quickly to customer issues
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - **For Cancellation Rate (<1%):**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Only list products you have in stock
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Update inventory regularly
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Ship all confirmed orders
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Avoid seller-initiated cancellations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - **For Ship on Time Rate (75%):**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Process orders within 24 hours
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Have efficient packing procedures
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Coordinate with logistics providers
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Set realistic handling times
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - **For Same Day Response Rate (70%):**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Check messages regularly throughout the day
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Respond to all customer inquiries within 24 hours
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Use quick reply templates for common questions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Enable notifications for new messages

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       ## Brand Relationship Types
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       ### Brand Owner
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - You own the trademark/brand
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Full control over brand usage
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Highest level of authorization
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Must provide proof of brand ownership
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - ### Exclusive Distributor
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Sole authorized distributor for the brand
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Exclusive selling rights in specific region/category
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Requires distributor authorization letter
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Must prove exclusive relationship
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - ### Non-Exclusive Distributor
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - One of multiple authorized distributors
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Non-exclusive selling rights
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Requires distributor authorization letter
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Shared distribution rights with others
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - ### Reseller
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Authorized to resell branded products
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - May have limited product range
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Requires reseller authorization or invoice proof
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Most common relationship type for retailers

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     ## Brand Authorization Requirements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     **Documentation Needed:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     **For Brand Owners:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - Trademark registration certificate
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Business license showing brand ownership
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Brand website or official documentation
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - **For Distributors (Exclusive & Non-Exclusive):**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Distributor authorization letter from brand owner
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Valid date range on authorization
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Specified product categories
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Brand owner's company stamp/signature
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - **For Resellers:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Reseller authorization letter OR
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Purchase invoices from authorized sources
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Valid authorization period
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Product category specifications

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         ## Tips and Best Practices
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         ### DarazMall Eligibility
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         1. **Monitor Regularly:** Check your performance metrics weekly
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         2. 2. **Proactive Improvement:** Don't wait until metrics drop below threshold
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            3. 3. **Balanced Performance:** Focus on all four metrics equally
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               4. 4. **Long-term Consistency:** Maintain standards over time, not just temporarily
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  5. 5. **Customer First:** Most metrics improve with better customer service
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     6. ### Brand Management
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     7. 1. **Keep Documentation Current:** Ensure authorization letters don't expire
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        2. 2. **Clear Relationships:** Only claim relationships you can prove
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           3. 3. **Update Promptly:** Inform Daraz if brand relationships change
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              4. 4. **Accurate Information:** Provide correct brand and category details
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 5. 5. **Respond to Reviews:** Address pending authorizations quickly if additional info requested
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    6. ### Authenticity Compliance
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    7. 1. **Source Verification:** Only sell genuine branded products
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       2. 2. **Documentation Ready:** Keep brand authorization accessible
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          3. 3. **Quality Control:** Inspect products before listing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             4. 4. **Transparent Sourcing:** Be able to prove product authenticity
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                5. 5. **Report Counterfeits:** Flag suspicious products from other sellers

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   7. ## Common Issues and Solutions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   8. ### Issue: DarazMall Eligibility Failed
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   9. **Solutions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   10. - Review which specific metrics are not meeting criteria
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Click "Performance Data Tips" for improvement guidance
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Focus on the metric(s) furthest from passing threshold
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Allow sufficient time (30-90 days) for metrics to improve
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Maintain consistent performance, not temporary spikes
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - ### Issue: Brand Not Showing in List
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - **Solutions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Check if correct filters are applied
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Click "Reset" to clear all filters
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Verify brand authorization was submitted and approved
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Check Review Result filter includes "Approved" status
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Ensure you're looking at the correct Brand Relation type
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - ### Issue: Cannot Find Specific Brand
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - **Solutions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Use the Brand Name search field
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Try partial brand name if exact name doesn't work
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Clear other filters that might be hiding results
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Check if brand is spelled correctly
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Verify brand authorization exists in system
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - ### Issue: Authorization Pending Too Long
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - **Solutions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Check if additional documentation was requested
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Review submission for completeness
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Ensure authorization letter includes all required information
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Contact Daraz support if pending over expected timeframe
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Verify brand owner information is correct
