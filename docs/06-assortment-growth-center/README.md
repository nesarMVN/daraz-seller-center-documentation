# Assortment Growth Center Documentation

## Overview

**URL:** https://sellercenter.daraz.com.bd/portal/assortment/workbench

**Navigation:** Assortment Growth → Assortment Growth Center

The Assortment Growth Center helps sellers optimize product assortment through three growth stages: finding best products, growing new product sales, and developing potential hero products. The center provides traffic boosting, benefits tracking, and guided tasks to accelerate product performance.

                                                                                                                                    - ## Workflow
                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                    - ### For New Products (Effective New Products)
                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                    - 1. **Identify Eligible Products** - Click Effective New Products section
                                                                                                                                      2. 2. **Complete Mandatory Task** - Enroll in Early Bird Price Discount
                                                                                                                                         3. 3. **Add Optional Tasks** - Join FSM and A+/Mega Campaign for maximum boost
                                                                                                                                            4. 4. **Monitor Performance** - Check Promoted tab and track L14D metrics
                                                                                                                                               5. 5. **Maintain Eligibility** - Keep tasks active to avoid Dropped out status
                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                  6. ### For Growing Products (PHP)
                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                  7. 1. **Review Eligible Products** - Click Potential Hero Products section
                                                                                                                                                     2. 2. **Complete All 4 Tasks:**
                                                                                                                                                        3.    - Ensure all SKUs in stock
                                                                                                                                                              -    - Join FSM program
                                                                                                                                                                   -    - Join A+/Mega Campaign
                                                                                                                                                                        -    - Enable Coins Discount
                                                                                                                                                                             - 3. **Monitor PHP Status** - Check progress bar and qualified products
                                                                                                                                                                               4. 4. **Leverage Benefits** - Use flash sale priority, traffic boost, campaign visibility
                                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                  5. ### Strategic Growth Path
                                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                  6. 1. Use Opportunity Center to find best products
                                                                                                                                                                                     2. 2. List products and enroll in Effective New Products for 3x traffic
                                                                                                                                                                                        3. 3. Graduate to PHP status by completing all requirements
                                                                                                                                                                                           4. 4. Future: Advance to Hero Product tier
                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                              5. ## Tips for Success
                                                                                                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                                                                                              6. ### Effective New Products
                                                                                                                                                                                              7. - Act fast on Early Bird Pricing (mandatory)
                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Stack benefits by completing all 3 tasks
                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Monitor L14D metrics closely
                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Maintain eligibility throughout boost period
                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Learn from real-time seller notifications
                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                         - ### PHP Benefits
                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Stock management is critical - no out-of-stock items
                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - FSM enrollment increases conversion
                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Campaign participation drives traffic
                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Set sustainable Coins discount percentage
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - All 4 tasks must be active simultaneously
                                                                                                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - ### Strategic Growth
                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Start with proven products from Opportunity Center
                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Use traffic boost to kickstart new listings
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Graduate successful products to PHP
                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Continuously optimize based on performance data
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Cycle through stages with new products
