# Reviews Management

## Overview

The Reviews Management system in Daraz Seller Center provides a comprehensive platform for managing customer reviews, monitoring review performance, and engaging with customers through review responses and questions. This feature is crucial for building trust, improving product quality, and enhancing overall seller reputation on the platform.

**URL:** https://sellercenter.daraz.com.bd/apps/review/management

**Navigation Path:** Daraz Seller Center → Orders and Reviews (sidebar) → Reviews

**Purpose:** Manage customer reviews, respond to feedback, analyze review performance, handle customer questions, and maintain high product ratings to drive sales.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - - **Impact:** Builds trust, improves seller rating

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - - **How:** Filter by rating, sort by date

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - - **Reference Specific Details:** Show you read their review
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - **Avoid Generic Templates:** Customize for each review
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - **Show Genuine Appreciation:** Be authentic

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - **Express Gratitude:** Simple thank you goes far

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      ## Common Issues and Solutions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      ### Issue 1: Low Response Rate to Reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Symptom:** Many reviews remain unanswered
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Causes:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Too busy with other tasks
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Forget to check reviews regularly
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Overwhelmed by volume
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Unclear who's responsible
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Solutions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Set Daily Schedule:** Allocate specific time for reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Calendar Reminders:** Daily alert at set time
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Assign Responsibility:** Designate team member
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Use Bulk Reply:** For similar reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Prioritize Critical:** Start with negative reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Track Metrics:** Monitor response rate weekly
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Prevention:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Build review management into daily routine
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Make it part of someone's job description
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Set response rate targets (e.g., 90% within 48 hours)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      ### Issue 2: Receiving Many Negative Reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Symptom:** High percentage of 1-2 star reviews
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Root Causes:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Product Quality Issues:** Defects or poor materials
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Description Mismatch:** Product differs from listing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Shipping Problems:** Damage or late delivery
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Wrong Expectations:** Customers misunderstand product
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Sizing Issues:** Clothes/shoes don't fit as expected
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      **Solutions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **Audit Product Quality:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Order sample yourself
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          - Compare to listing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - Test thoroughly
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - Check supplier reliability
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - **Update Listings:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - More accurate descriptions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - Better photos (all angles)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - Detailed specifications
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Size charts with measurements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Material details
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - **Improve Packaging:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          - Use protective materials
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - Double-box fragile items
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - Add fragile stickers
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - Include handling instructions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - **Set Realistic Expectations:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - Don't oversell features
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - Mention limitations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Provide actual photos
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Include scale references
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        **Prevention:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Source higher quality products
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Test before listing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Accurate marketing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Excellent packaging
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Fast, reliable shipping

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - Only if fundamental product changes made

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          - **Improve Continuously:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - Each product improvement matters
